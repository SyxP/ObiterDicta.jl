

Notice regarding Mirror Dungeon 2 Hard reward collection

Hello, this is Project Moon.

In the game's current version, playing Mirror Dungeon 2 hard after depleting the Mirror Dungeon 2 Hard Reward counts will disable the button to collect rewards.

With a future update, we plan to change it so that completing Mirror Dungeon 2 Hard after depleting its reward counts would still allow one to collect rewards from Mirror Dungeon 2 Normal instead.

We will provide you with a more concrete information regarding this update in near future with the scheduled updates.

We once again apologize for the inconvenience.
