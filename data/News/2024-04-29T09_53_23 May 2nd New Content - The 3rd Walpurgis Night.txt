※ Notice: Extraction Rate Changes during the Event Period
  During the Walpurgis Night Event,

  Due to this addition, the Extraction rates for certain Extractions will be changed while the Walpurgis Night event is active.

  we would like to inform you about the changing Extraction rates via this notice.

  (This information can also be viewed via [The 2nd Walpurgis Night Event] Notice from the Jan. 11th, 2024 Steam Announcements Page)
  
  Please note this information as you play the game.

5/2 Linked Content - Into a Certain Library’s Book

During the Event period, linked content
[Into a Certain Library’s Book] will be introduced to the game.

- Event Period : 5/2 12:00 ~ 5/16 10:00 (KST)
- Event Reward Exchange Period : 5/2 12:00 ~ 5/23 10:00 (KST)
- Unlock Conditions : None

<Description>
- With the start of the Event, the [Into a Certain Library’s Book]
   Event page will be added to the game.
- Complete special Missions(challenges) in the Easy and Hard
   difficulty Encounters to receive Mission Rewards.

<Event Progression>
- The 3rd Walpurgis Night - Into a Certain Library’s Book] Event
   can be accessed regardless of the Main Story progress.
- There are two difficulties to the ‘Into a Certain Library’s Book’
   Encounter: Easy and Hard.
- Completing the ‘Into a Certain Library’s Book’ Encounters
   will consume an Enkephalin Module. Clear the Easy difficulty
   to unlock the Hard difficulty.
- In ‘Easy’ difficulty, if your Identity’s Level is below 45,
   it will be raised to 45. Also, if your Identity’s Uptie Level is below
   Tier 3, it will be raised to Tier 3. However, your current E.G.O
   Threadspinning Tier will be reflected.

- During the Event Period, fulfill Missions(challenges) in each of
   the Easy and Hard difficulties to obtain their Rewards
   from the ‘Missions’ menu.

<Event Team Loadout>

- The 1st Sinner to be deployed in the Teams menu will be imbued with the 'Librarian of History' effect, which will vary depending on which difficulty Encounter you are running. The Sinner with the 'Librarian of History' effect will gain more Skill Slots and gain other beneficial effects.

<Event Combat Encounter>

- 'Into a Certain Library’s Book' Encounter is a Suppression of a ‘Fairy Festival’ Abnormality.

- Suppress the Abnormality, complete various missions, and earn various Event Rewards from them.

- However, going past 99 Turns will result in defeat.

<Missions>

- View Missions(challenges) to complete in the [Missions] menu.
