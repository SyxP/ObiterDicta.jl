If you have encountered a transaction-related error on the Steam version, please send us an email following the procedure below:

1. Go to [Account Details]
2. Go to [View Purchase History] on the Store & Purchase History tab
3. Select the erroneous purchase from the history
4. Check the transaction ID
5. Send an email to limbussupport@projmoon.com with the transaction ID clearly included and indicated

We apologize for the inconvenience.
